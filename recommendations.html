Taipei

Taipei 101

A global architectural icon, stands tall as the heart of Taipei, blending modern design with traditional Taiwanese elements. Once the world's tallest building, this skyscraper offers breathtaking views from its observation deck, reaching the cityscape and beyond. Visitors can also explore its luxury shopping mall, fine dining restaurants, and marvel at its innovative engineering, including the massive tuned mass damper that stabilizes the building.
